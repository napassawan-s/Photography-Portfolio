About me

Hello!

Welcome to Napa Photography

Napassawan, or Tien as most people calls, is a 22 years old girl who has a strong passion for the beauty of nature and moments of lives around her. She was born in her hometown, Taksin in 1999. Her passion on photography were sparked when she was 10 years old while she was looking over many beautiful sceneries through her father's camera. She started by learning from her father and developed from there. After graduate from high school, she went to Chulalongkorn university in Film and Still Photography in 2018 and developed another field of interest in photography along the way which is the portrait photography.

Gaining her experiences and skills through her time in the university, she started her career at the age of 20 as a freelance photographer and name her branding Napa Photography which is now still running and growing.
